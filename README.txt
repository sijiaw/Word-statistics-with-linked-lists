This program could read words from standard input, count their frequencies and store in a sorted linked list..
A word starts with a letter followed by by letters and/or digits. All other characters terminate words and can not be a part of a word.

 
Sample input: I am hungry.I want some chocolate.
       Onput: 
     	      I  2
             am  1
      chocolate  1
         hungry  1
           some  1
           want  1
